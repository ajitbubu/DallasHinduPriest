About Us

Who I Am

Vinod Shastri has been immersed in the study and practice of Hindu religious rituals and Karmakand from a young age. He began his training at the Shiv Kashi Sunderbani Ashram, where he developed a deep understanding of Vedic practices. He also completed his education in Sanskrit, earning a degree of Shashtri/Acharya from Sanskrit Hindu University in Varanasi in 2007.

Throughout his studies, Vinod Shastri gained practical experience conducting various Pujas with authentic Vedic rituals. His experience and knowledge of Karmakand are extensive, having started practicing in 1991. He also has a profound interest in Astrology and possesses deep knowledge in this field.

Vinod Shastri has served as a priest in many temples across India, including Shiv Kashi Ashram in Sunderbani, Maruti Mandir in Varanasi, and the Krishna Temple in J&K. He also served as a priest at DFW Hindu Temple from 2009 to 2021. In addition to his priestly duties, Vinod Shastri is also a talented singer and enjoys singing bhajans, kirtans, and kathas in honor of Mata Ji Jagran.
