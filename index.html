Services

Our service includes

Vinod Shastri experienced and knowledgeable Hindu priest offer a wide range of services to meet your needs. Whether you're looking to perform a traditional wedding ceremony, organize a puja or havan, or seek guidance and blessings for a new venture, we are here to help. We can also perform special ceremonies for festivals and other occasions.

Puja

Puja is a Hindu ritual that involves worshiping a god or deity to seek blessings and guidance for a happy and prosperous life.

Havan

Havan or Homam, is a Hindu ritual of offering oblations to the fire god, Agni, in order to seek blessings and purification.

Marriage

In Hindu culture, marriage is considered a sacred union between two individuals that involves various rituals and traditions.

House Warming

The Griha Pravesh or Housewarming Puja is a ritual that is performed to mark the entry into a new house for the first time.
